About me

I grew up near the beach, and I've been surfing for over 10 years, which has given me a deep appreciation for the ocean and the outdoors. Music is another one of my passions, and I've been playing guitar, bass, and drums for several years. I love playing with other musicians and performing live in front of an audience. Programming is also a significant interest of mine. I discovered my talent for it during college, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Since then, I've honed my skills in various programming languages and development tools, and I currently work for a tech startup that creates software for managing and analyzing large amounts of data. When I'm not working, surfing, or playing music, I love experimenting with new programming languages and tools, and working on side projects that challenge me creatively. Overall, I'm a driven and creative person who values a well-rounded and fulfilling life.
